# Section: Footer

## Layout
- Full-bleed black background.
- Left side: logo ("ZYLORA"), short descriptive line about newsletter value prop, email signup row (input field + solid purple "Submit" button with arrow), fine-print consent line below input.
- Right side: two link columns — "Company" (Home, Our Work) and "Follow Us" (LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram — each with platform icon).
- Horizontal divider line below both columns.
- Bottom-most row: copyright line, centered or left-aligned, small muted text.

## Content structure
- Newsletter pitch: "Join thousands of brand owners and marketers who get our weekly insights on email, SMS, and retention."
- Input placeholder: generic email example format.
- Consent microcopy: privacy policy + marketing consent disclosure (legally-oriented boilerplate, appropriately small/muted).
- Footer nav: minimal — only 2 company links, suggesting this is a lean single-product marketing site rather than a large multi-page site.
- Social links: 3 platforms only (LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram) — no Twitter/X or Facebook, consistent with earlier social-proof sections (Instagram icons on testimonial cards, YouTube videos section).

## Design notes
- Solid black footer is the darkest moment on the page, used only twice total (here and the dashboard-feature section) — reserved for "final/serious" moments rather than overused.
- Consent microcopy presence is a good practice detail (GDPR/CAN-SPAM-style compliance signal) worth keeping in any real newsletter signup form.

## Reference-only note
Minimal footer nav (2 links) works because this is a single-purpose landing page. A different site with more pages/content would need a fuller footer — copy the density-level appropriate to the actual site being built, not just this page's specific 2 links.
