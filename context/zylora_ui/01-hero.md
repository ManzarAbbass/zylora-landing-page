# Section: Hero

## Layout
- Nav: logo left ("ZYLORA"), 6 links center (About us, Testimonials, Our Work, Designs, Our AI System, FAQs), solid purple CTA button top-right with arrow.
- Below nav: small floating pill badge left-aligned ("OVER 35+ EDUCATIONAL VIDEOS ON YOUTUBE").
- Large serif headline left, 3 lines, one word italicized in purple accent ("Email").
- Subtext paragraph below headline (1-2 lines), one word ("guarantee") highlighted in purple.
- Solid purple CTA button below subtext, same style as nav CTA.
- Right side: collage of tilted rectangular image cards (product/email mockups) fanned out, floating badge chips overlaid on collage ("Bespoke Designs — By real graphic designers", "Real reporting — Based off brand data"), small decorative arrow doodles (purple squiggle) around collage.

## Content structure
- Headline: "Boutique *Email* Agency That Takes Your Growth Seriously"
- Subtext: value prop sentence with "guarantee" as trust word.
- CTA: "Get a Free Email Audit"

## Design notes
- Palette: white bg, black/dark serif headline text, purple (#6366F1 -ish) accent + CTA buttons, light gray pill badges with black text.
- Typography: serif for headline (italic for emphasis word), sans-serif for nav/body/buttons.
- Decorative hand-drawn arrow doodles used as accents pointing at collage elements — signals playful/boutique tone.
