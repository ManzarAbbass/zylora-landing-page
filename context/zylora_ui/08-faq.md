# Section: FAQ

## Layout
- Full-bleed purple rounded section, faint repeating wordmark background pattern (consistent with sections 2 and 6).
- Centered serif heading: "Frequently Asked *Questions*" (italic yellow accent on second word).
- Vertically stacked accordion list, 5 items, white rounded-pill rows, question text left, "+" expand icon right (none expanded in this screenshot — default collapsed state).

## Content structure
- 5 questions, each addressing a specific objection/risk a prospective client would raise before buying:
  1. Will I be handed over to freelancers? (trust/quality concern)
  2. How do I know you can do what you say you can? (credibility/proof concern)
  3. Do I need an email provider? (practical prerequisite)
  4. What do I need to do? (effort/involvement expectation)
  5. Will email marketing work for me? (fit/suitability doubt)

## Design notes
- Objection-handling order matters: starts with trust concerns, moves to practical logistics, ends with the broadest doubt ("will this even work for me") — a deliberate objection-ladder rather than random ordering.
- Accordion rows use full pill/rounded-rect shape (not just underlined text rows) — matches the soft, friendly boutique-brand visual language established in hero doodles and rounded cards throughout.

## Reference-only note
The specific questions here are generic to any service business — reusable as a *starting checklist* of objection types to cover (trust, credibility, prerequisites, effort, fit), but actual answers must be written to be true for whatever business/project this gets adapted for.
